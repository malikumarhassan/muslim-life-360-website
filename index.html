Built with the community

Local Jumu'ah knowledge, shared

Community-contributed Jumu'ah sessions help worshippers find local Friday prayer options. When a mosque has no timetable, users can add a known time to help others.

Accuracy matters. Community times are not invented or calculated from Dhuhr. Always confirm a session with the local mosque when needed.
