Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

Muslim Life 360 is developed by Malik Umar Hassan.

Information the app uses

The app may request location access to calculate prayer times, show Qibla direction, and find nearby mosques and Jumu'ah venues. It may use compass and motion sensor data for Qibla guidance and request notification permission for prayer reminders.

Data collection

No account is required. Muslim Life 360 does not sell personal data. Location and compass information are used to provide app features. Reminder preferences and settings may be stored locally on your device.

Community-contributed Jumu'ah timings may be stored in a cloud database and shared with other app users. Do not submit personal or confidential information.

Third-party services

The app may open Apple Maps, Google Maps, or another maps service when you request directions. Those services operate under their own privacy policies.

Payments

Optional contributions are processed securely by Apple App Store or Google Play. Muslim Life 360 does not receive or store your full payment card details.

Retention and deletion

Local settings can be removed by deleting the app. Community Jumu'ah submissions may remain available to preserve shared timetable information. Contact the developer to request review or correction of submitted timetable data.

Children

The app is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, email malikumarhassan@live.com.
