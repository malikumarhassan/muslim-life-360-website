# Muslim Life 360 Social Media Kit

## Recommended handles

Use the same handle everywhere where available: `@MuslimLife360App`

Suggested profiles: Facebook Page, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube and LinkedIn Page.

## Short bio

Prayer times, azan reminders, Qibla guidance, nearby mosques and community Jumu'ah times. Free on iPhone and Android. Developed in Newcastle, Australia.

## Extended bio

Muslim Life 360 is a free daily prayer companion with location-based prayer times, configurable reminders, live Qibla guidance, nearby mosque discovery and community-contributed Jumu'ah sessions. Prepared by Malik Umar Hassan in Newcastle, NSW.

## Launch post

Introducing Muslim Life 360: one simple place for daily prayer times, azan reminders, Qibla guidance, nearby mosques and local Jumu'ah information.

The app is free to use, and every feature remains available without payment. Community members can also help one another by contributing known Jumu'ah session times for nearby mosques.

App Store and Google Play links will be shared when the public listings are live.

#MuslimLife360 #PrayerTimes #Qibla #Jumuah #MuslimApp #NewcastleNSW

## Feature post: Jumu'ah

Looking for a nearby Friday prayer? Muslim Life 360 helps you discover local mosques and Jumu'ah venues. When a session is missing, the community can contribute a known time to help others. Please always confirm community information with the mosque.

#Jumuah #FridayPrayer #MosqueFinder #MuslimCommunity

## Feature post: Qibla

Find the Qibla from your current location. Muslim Life 360 calculates the direction toward Masjid al-Haram using your device location and compass. For best results, calibrate your phone away from magnetic interference.

#Qibla #Makkah #MuslimLife360

## Support post

Muslim Life 360 is completely free. Optional one-time support helps maintain prayer tools, improve mosque listings and keep building useful features. Every feature remains available whether you contribute or not.

Contributions are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play, which shows the final local price before payment.
